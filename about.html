About

Honoring the stories of the Zomi people.

ZomiHub is a community-led initiative preserving films, music, books, and oral histories created by and for the Zomi people. We work with elders, historians, and artists to ensure every item is treated with care.

Our mission

Preserve, educate, and celebrate

Preservation

Digitizing archives using community-built standards validated with cultural stewards.

Education

Providing learning materials, language lessons, and curriculum guides to schools and families.

Celebration

Sharing music, films, and stories that showcase the creativity of Zomi communities worldwide.

Team

Stewards and contributors

Curatorial council

A rotating group of elders, artists, and researchers guiding archival priorities.

Technology partners

Engineers and designers building accessible tools for streaming, downloads, and metadata.

Community volunteers

Contributors recording oral histories, translating content, and hosting listening sessions.
